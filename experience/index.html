History

The Madrona framework evolved from MarineMap, a tool created to support stakeholders in designing marine protected areas for the Marine Life Protection Act Initiative in California. Madrona builds on the design and collaboration features of MarineMap, creating a framework that can be used for both marine and terrestrial planning in any region and at any scale.
